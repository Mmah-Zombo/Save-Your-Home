User Guide

Enter the location that you want weather data for and click the search button or the ENTER key on your key board.


Your display will change to a page with a search button and a list containing the results, of at most five places, that match the location you entered. By defualt the app displays the weather condition of the first location that matches your input.


To see the weather info for any of the listed locations click on it, and the info will be displayed in the content section below the search button


Note

- Country name codes are used instead of the country names. Example "Sierra Leone" becomes "SL".

- The speed of your internet affects the app's load time.
